Our Story

Born from a
Love for Beauty

Lumière Beauty was born in 2019 from a simple belief — every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in her own skin. Founded in Mumbai, our brand bridges the gap between global luxury and the unique needs of Indian skin — its diverse tones, its resilience, its brilliance.

We source the finest botanicals from across India — saffron from Kashmir, turmeric from Kerala, rose from Rajasthan — and marry them with cutting-edge cosmetic science to create formulas that are not just effective, but truly transformative.
